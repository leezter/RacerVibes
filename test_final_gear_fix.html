✅ FINAL FIX TEST: Consistent 1st & Top Gear

The Fix

Problem: Previous implementation used fixed spacing (1.28), which meant fewer gears skipped the low gears needed for acceleration from standstill.

Solution: Calculate spacing dynamically to always include:

  • 1st gear ratio ~3.5 (good for 0-20 m/s acceleration)
  • Top gear ratio ~1.03 (reaches ~64 m/s at redline)
  • Evenly spaced gears in between
